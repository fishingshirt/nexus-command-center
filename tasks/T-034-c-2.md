# T-034-c-2 — Offline Queue Retry & Sync Status Gating

## Goal
Harden the outbound sync queue with retry limits and ensure the sync-status badge only shows `synced` after the outbound queue has been fully flushed.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Rename `ncc-cal-outbound-queue` to `ncc-calendar-sync-queue` in `gcal-outbound.js` (single key change).
- [ ] Queue items store `retryCount` (default 0).
- [ ] `flushOutboundQueue()` increments `retryCount` on failure, removes items that reach `retryCount >= 3`, and reports them as permanently failed.
- [ ] Replace the `synced` status toast/badge in `gcal-sync.js` with a two-phase status:
  - After inbound pull completes → status = `syncing` (badge text: "Merging…").
  - After `flushOutboundQueue()` finishes AND queue is empty → status = `synced`.
- [ ] On `nexusOnline`, trigger both inbound sync and outbound flush.
- [ ] `saveStatus()` accepts an optional `phase` param (`inbound` | `outbound`) to differentiate phases.

## Files to Touch
- `public/js/apps/gcal-outbound.js` — rename key, add retryCount logic.
- `public/js/apps/gcal-sync.js` — update `saveStatus()`, call flush after inbound merge.
- `public/js/apps/calendar.js` — wire `nexusOnline` to trigger flush.

## `localStorage` Keys
- `ncc-calendar-sync-queue`: `[{ action, payload, retryCount, timestamp }]`
