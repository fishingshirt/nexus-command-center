## Goal

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] On wake, check if `newsHub.aiDigestEnabled == true`.

- [ ] On ready, send notification toast via notifications center: "📰 Your daily news digest is ready."
- [ ] Persist last digest content in `localStorage` (`ncc-news-last-digest`).
