## Architecture
- **Backend:** `scripts/nexus-server.py` adds `/api/gmail/oauth/start`, `/api/gmail/oauth/callback`, `/api/gmail/status`, `/api/gmail/unlink`, `/api/gmail/threads`, `/api/gmail/send`, `/api/gmail/draft`
- **Frontend:** `public/js/apps/email.js` registers in app registry; `public/css/apps/email.css` for inbox layout
- **Storage prefix:** `ncc-email-*` for local cache; `~/.hermes/nexus-gmail-tokens.json` for OAuth tokens
- **Dependencies:** Reuses calendar OAuth credential loading from `~/.hermes/.env`
